About Us

Transforming Spaces Since 2020

At Shiny Space Cleaners, we don't just clean — we transform your environment into a spotless, refreshing, and hygienic space you'll love. Founded with a passion for cleanliness and an eye for detail, we specialize in residential, commercial, and post-construction cleaning services.

Our trained and trustworthy team uses eco-friendly products, modern equipment, and a personalized approach to ensure every corner shines. Whether it's your home, office, or special project, we deliver reliable, top-quality cleaning tailored to your needs and schedule.

Our Policies

Transparent guidelines for quality service delivery

Appointment & Scheduling

  • Appointments must be scheduled at least 24 hours in advance
  • Flexible scheduling: daily, weekly, biweekly, or monthly
  • Confirmation sent 12–24 hours before scheduled time
  • Late rescheduling or cancellations (less than 12 hours) may incur a fee

Supplies & Equipment

  • We provide all standard cleaning supplies and equipment
  • Clients may request use of their preferred cleaning products
  • Eco-friendly and non-toxic products used by default

Health & Safety

  • Strict hygiene and safety protocols followed at all times
  • Cleaners wear protective gear as needed
  • Unwell team members will not report to duty

Privacy & Security

  • Client information and property treated with confidentiality
  • Staff professionally trained and thoroughly vetted
